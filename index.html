About Me

I am Zachariah, I am a Computer Science student who has seeked refuge from my own professional enviroment after being engulfed by technology and the burdens it can bear. Trekking the trails leading into nature have not been a hobby I've entrenched myself deep in until late years, but it is the one that serves me the most peace. Retreating into the mountains and forests allowed my head a reset.

More Description

"What adventure is there to have, if we do not take risks? To live is to risk it all, and controversially, even in the face of death.
